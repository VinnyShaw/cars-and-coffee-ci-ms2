What

Cars and Coffee Inverness is monthly automotive event where like minded car fans get together to show off their cars and socialize.
Image to go here

When

The main event is held on the 2nd Sunday of each month, between 10am and 12pm.
There are also regular organized "Drive It" days, held on pre-defined routes around the Inverness area.
Image to go here

Where

Each event is held at Rollerbowl, 167 Culduthel Road, Inverness.
